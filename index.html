Questions

Frequently asked.

Everything families want to know before starting.

What age range is the program designed for?

NeuroHome is designed for children ages 3‚Äì17 on the autism spectrum. Our clinicians specialize in neurodevelopmental conditions and tailor each session to your child's specific needs and developmental stage.

Do I need any technical setup at home?

Just a stable internet connection and a quiet space. We ship the neurofeedback device pre-configured with instructions and provide tech support before every session. Most families are set up within 15 minutes.

How much does the program cost?

The pilot program is private pay. We're building ESA funding navigation to help families in 18 states cover costs through Education Savings Accounts. Book a consult for current pricing details.

What is the ATEC assessment?

The Autism Treatment Evaluation Checklist (ATEC) measures speech, sociability, sensory/cognitive awareness, and health/behavior. We use it at baseline and monthly to objectively track your child's progress.

Is this available outside of Georgia?

We're launching our pilot with Georgia families first. Expansion to Florida, North Carolina, Arizona, and Tennessee is planned for the next phase alongside ESA funding support.

Who are the clinicians?

All clinicians are certified through Dr. Kyle Daigle's NeuroSage Hub training program. They are licensed professionals (BCBAs, OTs, SLPs) trained on the exact methodology used at NeuroSolution Center of Atlanta.
